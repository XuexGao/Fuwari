Next, we will expand the capacity.

![](../../assets/images/ms-e3-5.webp)

At this moment, your account will be automatically scaled up. If you can't wait, we can manually force the scaling up.

Go back to the Management Center, select Users - Active Users - Manage Roles - OneDrive, and force expansion

# Formally begin

> This tutorial is for getting started with Umami Cloud. If you need to self-host Umami, please search for the relevant documentation yourself.

Enter https://umami.is/ (if you cannot access it, please check whether an ad blocker is blocking Umami!!!)

Click the `Sign UP` in the top right corner to register an account

![](../../assets/images/3486d3c9-ab00-46d2-b8bf-4916fe8045bf.webp)

Fill in the relevant information as needed to register.

![](../../assets/images/adcb3e73-bd1d-4c6d-9ad2-c6ba2b17441d.webp)

![](../../assets/images/f6ff9d8a-e281-4f36-a917-686264479a5d.webp)

Then check your email to see if you have received the email verification message from Umami.

![](../../assets/images/50877413-0dcf-4229-b388-dc0ae4634414.webp)

Click `Login` to log in and enter the verification code

![](../../assets/images/29321cee-f0a7-4d3b-9d1b-b507d34d3958.webp)

Region selection is arbitrary (choosing the United States means your statistical data will be stored on servers in the United States; choosing the EU means your statistical data will be stored on servers in the EU).

![](../../assets/images/e7d5daaa-032a-4587-bd68-3d4194e88555.webp)

The user configuration can be filled in.

![](../../assets/images/b22a2c6b-43a0-4da8-b7f7-25b58f9118e7.webp)

Continue filling in the websites you want to track.

![](../../assets/images/bf3b74be-26d7-4618-8b22-72cab03ec22f.webp)

Insert the tracking code into the `<head>Umami tracking code</head>` of your website

![](../../assets/images/e58f4825-573c-4faf-8619-79c5eb194abe.webp)

Then we enter the Umami console.

![](../../assets/images/6318e324-5a2a-47c8-8ab9-982131f2eb5b.webp)

Click `View` to see your website statistics.

![](../../assets/images/8ab369ca-767e-4783-a38f-35f8ae59c654.webp)

Note: The top right corner can be changed to Chinese.

![](../../assets/images/9f32167c-d522-41a2-805b-202a97e91dfe.webp)

It is recommended to enable the Share URL setting in your settings, so your users can see your site's traffic in real time.

![](../../assets/images/21681684-8a26-4d15-8d3e-c51183febaf0.webp)
